Our 4-Phase Operating Model

Tender-friendly process with clear phases, deliverables, and governance. We show how we work, not just claim expertise.

01

Assess (2–3 Weeks)

Comprehensive discovery, risk review, and implementation of quick wins to stabilize your environment immediately.

  • Full audit of current IT infrastructure, security posture, and compliance gaps.
  • Risk identification including vulnerabilities in identity, endpoints, email, and devices.
  • Quick wins: Immediate fixes to high-impact issues like patching critical vulnerabilities.
Key Deliverables

Current state report, risk register, and prioritized roadmap outline.

02

Roadmap

Detailed 30/60/90-day uplift plan with transparent effort estimates, costs, and expected outcomes.

  • Prioritization based on risk, business impact, and ROI.
  • Alignment with standards like CIS benchmarks and NIST frameworks.
  • Incorporation of enterprise best practices for security (e.g., Zero Trust) and compliance.
Key Deliverables

Phased uplift plan, budget breakdown, and measurable KPIs (e.g., ticket reduction targets).

03

Uplift

Implementation of baseline controls, standardization, and noise reduction to build a solid foundation.

  • Deploy enterprise-grade tools (e.g., Entra ID, Intune, Proofpoint) without bloat.
  • Standardize processes: Change control, patch management, DR testing.
  • Reduce reactive work by addressing root causes proactively.
Key Deliverables

Implemented controls, standardized documentation (asset register, diagrams, escalation paths), and initial outcome metrics.

04

Run

Ongoing managed service with continuous optimization and reporting.

  • Proactive monitoring, preventive maintenance, and AI-driven insights.
  • Quarterly improvements based on evolving threats and business needs.
  • Compliance assurance with regular audits and reporting.
Key Deliverables

Monthly reports, quarterly roadmap reviews, and sustained reductions in tickets and risks.

Governance Rhythm

Structured cadence ensures transparency and alignment, especially for public-sector tenders.

  • Weekly Ops Calls
    Review ongoing activities, address immediate concerns, and track progress.
  • Monthly Service Reviews
    Deep dive into metrics, SLAs (e.g., patch deployment), and performance against KPIs.
  • Quarterly Roadmap Updates
    Strategic review, adjustments, and planning for continuous optimization.

Documentation & Standards

We maintain rigorous standards to ensure repeatability and audit-readiness.

  • Documentation Standards
    Comprehensive asset registers, network diagrams, admin account inventories, and escalation paths.
  • Change Control Approach
    Lightweight yet robust process: Review, approval, testing, and rollback plans for all changes—public sector approved.
  • Compliance Alignment
    CIS baselines, NIST-aligned approaches, patch SLAs, and regular DR testing.
