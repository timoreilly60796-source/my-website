M365 governance is a scored criterion in most ICT compliance frameworks

Public sector and healthcare procurement processes increasingly include Microsoft 365 governance as a scored requirement. This covers documented data retention and classification policies, evidence of SharePoint and Teams governance, MFA enforcement records, and Exchange Online compliance configuration. A poorly governed M365 environment will cost points on the governance assessment - or disqualify a submission entirely.
