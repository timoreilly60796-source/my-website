How We Work

A Proven Four-Step Methodology

Structured. Transparent. Results-driven.

01

Assess (2–3 Weeks)

Comprehensive discovery, risk review, and implementation of quick wins to stabilise your environment immediately.

  • Full audit of current IT infrastructure, security posture, and compliance gaps.
  • Risk identification including vulnerabilities in identity, endpoints, email, and devices.
  • Quick wins: immediate fixes to high-impact issues like patching critical vulnerabilities.
Key Deliverables

Current state report, risk register, and prioritised roadmap outline.

02

Roadmap

Detailed 30/60/90-day uplift plan with transparent effort estimates, costs, and expected outcomes.

  • Prioritisation based on risk, business impact, and ROI.
  • Alignment with standards like CIS benchmarks and NIST frameworks.
  • Incorporation of enterprise best practices for security and compliance.
Key Deliverables

Phased uplift plan, budget breakdown, and measurable KPIs (e.g., ticket reduction targets).

03

Uplift

Implementation of baseline controls, standardisation, and noise reduction to build a solid foundation.

  • Deploy enterprise-grade tools without unnecessary bloat or complexity.
  • Standardise processes: change control, patch management, DR testing.
  • Reduce reactive work by addressing root causes proactively.
Key Deliverables

Implemented controls, standardised documentation, asset register, and initial outcome metrics.

04

Run

Ongoing managed service with continuous optimisation and reporting — your IT in safe hands.

  • Proactive monitoring, preventive maintenance, and AI-driven insights.
  • Quarterly improvements based on evolving threats and business needs.
  • Compliance assurance with regular audits and transparent reporting.
Key Deliverables

Monthly reports, quarterly roadmap reviews, and sustained reductions in tickets and risk.
