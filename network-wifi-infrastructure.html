A network built by an ISP engineer and never revisited is not a managed network

Most SME networks were set up when the business moved into its premises, configured by whoever installed the broadband, and never touched since. Without VLAN segmentation, guest and corporate traffic share the same subnet. Without monitoring, outages are discovered by users rather than alerts. Without documentation, every troubleshooting session starts from zero.

No network documentation - topology unknown, switch ports unlabelled, VLAN config undocumented
Guest and corporate Wi-Fi on the same subnet with no segmentation
Network hardware approaching or past end of life with no replacement plan in place
Wi-Fi dead zones in parts of the building affecting productivity and call quality
